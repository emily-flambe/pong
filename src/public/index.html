4-PADDLE PONG CONTROLS

Arrow Keys Control:

  • ↑↓ Up/Down Arrows - Move Left & Right paddles vertically
  • ←→ Left/Right Arrows - Move Top & Bottom paddles horizontally

WASD Alternative:

  • W/S Keys - Move Left & Right paddles vertically
  • A/D Keys - Move Top & Bottom paddles horizontally

Game Rules:

  • Start with 3 lives
  • Lose a life when ball hits any wall
  • Score +1 point when ball hits any paddle
  • All paddles move together in synchronized pairs
  • ENTER key starts game, SPACEBAR pauses

Press Start or ENTER to begin your 4-paddle challenge!
